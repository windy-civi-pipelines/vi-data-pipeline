Section 1:
COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE/FLOOR
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
July 10, 2025
An act amending Official Zoning Map No. STZ-11, St. Thomas to rezone Parcel No. 100-1C
Estate Taarneberg, King’s Quarter, St. Thomas from R-3 (Residential-Medium Density) to B-
1 (Business-Central Business District)
PROPOSED BY: Senator Dwayne M. DeGraff
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Pursuant to title 29 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 3, section 238, Official
3 Zoning Map No. STZ-11 for the island of St. Thomas is amended by changing the zoning
4 designation of Parcel No. 100-1C Estate Taarneberg, King’s Quarter, St. Thomas consisting of
5 2.00 U.S. acres, as shown on survey drawing D9-8595-T011 from R-3 (Residential-Medium
6 Density) to B-1 (Business-Central Business District).
7 BILL SUMMARY
8 This bill provides for the rezoning of Parcel No. 100-1C Estate Taarneberg, King’s
9 Quarter, St. Thomas.
10 BR25-0667/June 26, 2025/PFA
