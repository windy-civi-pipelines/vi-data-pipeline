Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON BUDGET, APPROPRIATIONS AND FINANCE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
May 19, 2025
An act appropriating $1,500,000 from the General Fund of the Treasury of the Government of
the Virgin Islands to the Virgin Islands Diabetes Center of Excellence for staffing and
operations within the Territory
PROPOSED BY: Senator Ray Fonseca
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. (a) The sum of $1,500,000 is appropriated in the fiscal year ending
3 September 30, 2026, from the General Fund of the Treasury of the Government of the Virgin
4 Islands to the Virgin Islands Diabetes Center of Excellence for staffing and operations in the
5 Territory.
6 (b) The sum appropriated in subsection (a) remains available until expended.
7 BILL SUMMARY
8 This bill provides an appropriation from the General Fund of the Treasury of the
9 Government of the Virgin Islands to the Virgin Islands Diabetes Center of Excellence for
10 staffing and operations in the Territory.
