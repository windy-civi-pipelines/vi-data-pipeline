Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON BUDGET, APPROPRIATIONS AND FINANCE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
March 28, 2025
An Act providing an appropriation from the General Fund of the Treasury of the Government
of the Virgin Islands to the Governor Juan F. Luis Hospital and Medical Center and to the Roy
L. Schneider Hospital and Medical Center to address medical staff needs
P ROPOSED BY: Senator Ray Fonseca
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. (a) The sum of $2,000,000 is appropriated in the fiscal year ending
3 September 30, 2025 from the General Fund of the Treasury of the Government of the Virgin
4 Islands to the Governor Juan F. Luis Hospital and Medical Center to hire seven emergency
5 room nurses, two emergency room physicians, and seven emergency room patient care
6 technicians. The sum appropriated remains available until expended.
7 (b) The sum of $2,000,000 is appropriated in the fiscal year ending September 30, 2025
8 from the General Fund of the Treasury of the Government of the Virgin Islands to the Roy L.
9 Schneider Hospital and Medical Center to hire seven emergency room nurses, two emergency
10 room physicians, and seven emergency room patient care technicians. The sum appropriated
11 remains available until expended.
12 BILL SUMMARY
13 This bill appropriates from the General Fund of the Treasury of the Government of the
14 Virgin Islands $2,000,000 to the Governor Juan F. Luis Hospital and Medical Center and
15 $2,000,000 to the Schneider Regional Hospital and Medical Center to address medical staff
16 needs.
17 BR25-0255/February 27, 2025/CBM
