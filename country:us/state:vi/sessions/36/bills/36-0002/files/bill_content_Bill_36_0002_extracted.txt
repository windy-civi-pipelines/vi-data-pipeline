Section 1:
FLOOR
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
January 13, 2025
A Resolution adopting the Rules of the 36th Legislature of the Virgin Islands
PROPOSED BY: Senators Milton E. Potter, Kenneth L. Gittens, Avery L. Lewis,
Kurt A. Vialet, Marvin A. Blyden, Angel L. Bolques, Jr., Novelle E.
Francis, Jr., Hubert L. Frederick, Ray Fonseca, Carla J. Joseph,
Clifford A. Joseph, Sr., and Marise C. James
1 WHEREAS, on January 13, 2025, the Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands was
2 organized through the adoption of Bill No. 36-0001;
3 WHEREAS, it is axiomatic that every legislative body has an inherent right regulate its
4 own procedure for the conduct of the business before it;
5 WHEREAS, pursuant to section 6 of the Revised Organic Act of the Virgin Islands, 48
6 U.S.C. § 1572(b), the Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands has and may exercise all
7 the authority and attributes, inherent in legislative assemblies and has the power to adopt, alter,
8 amend, or repeal rules of procedure; and
9 WHEREAS, in order to promote and maintain the orderly and proper consideration of
10 the people’s business that comes before it, the Thirty-Sixth Legislature intends to adopt the
2
1 rules set forth in the document entitled Rules of the 36th Legislature of the Virgin Islands; Now
2 Therefore,
3 Be it resolved by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
4 SECTION 1. Pursuant section 6(g) of the Revised Organic Act of the Virgin Islands, 48
5 U.S. § 1572(g), the Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands adopts the rules governing
6 the organization, procedure, and operation of the Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands,
7 as set forth in the document titled “Rules of the 36th Legislature of the Virgin Islands”, dated
8 January 13, 2025, which is attached as Appendix A to this Resolution and by this reference is
9 made a part of this Resolution.
10 BILL SUMMARY
11 This bill adopts the Rules of the 36th Legislature of the Virgin Islands.
12 BR25-0002/January 13, 2025/AWC JR
