Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND
AGRICULTURE
04/16/2025-HELD IN COMMITTEE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
February 27, 2025
An Act amending title 12A Virgin Islands Code, chapter 2, subchapter I, section 101 to prohibit
the imposition of a surcharge for the use of a credit or debit card
P ROPOSED BY: Senators Marvin A. Blyden and Angel L. Bolques, Jr.
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 12A Virgin Islands Code, chapter 2, subchapter I, section 101 is
3 amended by designating the existing language as subsection (a) and inserting the following
4 subsections (b), (c) and (d):
5 “(b) No person, merchant, corporation or vendor shall impose a surcharge on a
6 consumer who chooses to use a credit card instead of cash, check or any similar means of
7 payment in any transaction that involves the sale or lease of products and services.
8 (c) Merchants may offer discounts to promote payment in cash, check or any other
9 similar method that does not involve the use of a credit card, provided that the discount is
10 offered to all potential buyers.
11 (d) For purposes of this section the term “surcharge” means any increase in the regular
12 price of a product or service imposed on the client by the merchant or lessor when using a
13 credit card as a method of payment.”
2
1 BILL SUMMARY
2 This bill prohibits the imposition of a surcharge for the use of a credit or debit card in the
3 purchase of goods or services.
4 BR25-0210/February 18, 2025/AA/Revised/February 26, 2025
