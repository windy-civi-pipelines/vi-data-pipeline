Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON GOVERNMENT OPERATIONS, VETERANS
AFFAIRS AND CONSUMER PROTECTION
08/08/2025-HELD IN COMMITTEE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
March 28, 2025
An Act amending title 18 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 1, section 4 and title 18 Virgin Islands
Code, chapter 3, section 41 relating to the powers of the Supervisor of Elections and the Board
of Elections
PROPOSED BY: Senator Alma Francis Heyliger
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 18 Virgin Islands Code is amended as follows:
3 (a) chapter 1, section 4:
4 (1) subsection (a) is amended in the seventh sentence by striking “shall be
5 entitled to” and inserting “may” by striking “an adverse ruling of” and inserting “their
6 removal” and by striking “the Board”;
7 (2) subsection (b) is amended in the introductory phrase by striking “boards of
8 elections” and inserting “Board”;
9 (3) subsection (b), paragraph (2) is amended by striking “boards of elections”
10 and inserting “Board”; and
11 (4) By adding the following subsection (c):
2
1 “(c) Notwithstanding the powers granted to the Supervisor of Elections
2 under subsection (b), the Board may overrule any decision of the Supervisor of
3 Elections at an official meeting of the Board.”
4 (b) chapter 3, section 41, subsection (h) is amended in first sentence by striking “each
5 Board shall be” and inserting “the Board is” and in the second sentence by striking “a” where
6 it appears before “Board” and inserting “the”.
7 BILL SUMMARY
8 This bill amends title 18 Virgin Islands Code, section 4 to clarify that the Supervisor of
9 Elections and the Deputy Supervisors of election may appeal their removal to the Superior
10 Court of the Virgin Islands, that there is a singular Board of Elections and not two district Board
11 of Elections and gives the Board of Elections explicit authority to overrule decisions of the
12 Supervisor of Elections. Finally, it also amends section 41 to clarify that there is a singular
13 Board of Elections.
