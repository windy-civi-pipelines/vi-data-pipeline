Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON RULES AND JUDICIARY
10/23/2025-REPORTED OUT TO THE FLOOR
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
August 26, 2025
An act amending title 3 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 22, section 415 to add a term of service
for appointees to the Board of Physical Therapy
PROPOSED BY: Senator Avery L. Lewis
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 3 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 23, section 415, subsection (b),
3 paragraph (6), subparagraph (A), is amended by adding at the end of the subparagraph: “All
4 board members are appointed to a three year term, and may serve until their successor is
5 appointed.”
6 BILL SUMMARY
7 This bill adds a three year term of service for members of the Physical Therapy Board.
8 BR25-0646B/July 23, 2025/LMW
