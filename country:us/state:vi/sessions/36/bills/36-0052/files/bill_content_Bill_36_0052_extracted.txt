Section 1:
AND CONSUMER PROTECTION
BILL NO. 36-0052
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
March 28, 2025
An Act amending title 29, Virgin Islands Code, chapter 9, section 502, regarding advertising
devices on private property
PROPOSED BY: Senator Carla J. Joseph
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 29 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 9, section 502 is amended by striking
3 “, whether on public or private property,” and inserting “on public property”.”
4 BILL SUMMARY
5 This bill amends title 29 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 9, section 502 to clarify that the
6 provision applies exclusively to public property.
7 BR25-0196/March 10, 2025/KEH
