Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON HOMELAND SECURITY, JUSTICE AND
PUBLIC SAFETY
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
June 24, 2025
An act amending title 33 Virgin Islands Code, subtitle 3, chapter 113, section 3109(b) to require
that violations committed by officers or employees of any governmental agency be reported to
the Attorney General of the Virgin Islands for investigation and prosecution
PROPOSED BY: Senator Carla J. Joseph
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 33 Virgin Islands Code, subtitle 3, chapter 113, section 3109(b) is
3 amended by striking “Governor, through the Director of the Office of Management and
4 Budget,” and inserting “Attorney General of the Virgin Islands for investigation and
5 prosecution, if warranted.”
6 BILL SUMMARY
7 This bill amends title 33 of the Virgin Islands Code, subtitle 3, chapter 113, section
8 3109(b) by removing the requirement for agency heads to report violations to the Governor
9 through the Director of the Office of Management and Budget and instead reporting violations
10 to the Attorney General of the Virgin Islands.
11 BR25-0483/May 5, 2025/KEH
