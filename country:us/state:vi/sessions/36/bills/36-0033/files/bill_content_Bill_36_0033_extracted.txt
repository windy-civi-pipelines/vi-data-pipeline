Section 1:
COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE/FLOOR
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
February 27, 2025
An Act amending Official Zoning Map No. STZ-9 for the island of St. Thomas to rezone
Remainder Parcel No. 19-1-2, Parcel No. 19-1-2-7 (Eastern portion), and Parcel No. 19-1-2-7
(Western portion), Estate Smith Bay, Nos. 1, 2, and 3 East End Quarter, St. Thomas, Virgin
Islands from A-2 (Agricultural Zone) to B-3 (Business Scattered)
PROPOSED BY: Senator Milton E. Potter, by Request of the Governor t
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Pursuant to title 29 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 3, section 238, Official
3 Zoning Map No. STZ-9 for the island of St. Thomas, Virgin Islands is amended by changing
4 the zoning designation of Remainder Parcel No. 19-1-2 Estate Smith Bay, Nos. 1, 2, and 3 East
5 End Quarter, St. Thomas, Virgin Islands, consisting of approximately 13.460 U.S. acres, as
6 described on survey drawing D9-9375-T019, from A-2 (Agricultural Zone) to B-3 (Business
7 Scattered); Parcel No. 19-1-2-7 (Eastern portion) Estate Smith Bay, Nos. 1, 2, and 3 East End
8 Quarter, St. Thomas, Virgin Islands, consisting of approximately 1.454 U.S. acres, as described
9 on survey drawing D9-9375-T019, from A-2 (Agricultural Zone) to B-3 (Business Scattered);
10 and Parcel No. 19-1-2-7 (Western portion) Estate Smith Bay, Nos. 1, 2, and 3 East End
11 Quarter, St. Thomas, Virgin Islands, consisting of approximately 0.846 U.S. acres, as described
12 on survey drawing D9-9375-T019, from A-2 (Agricultural Zone) to B-3 (Business Scattered).
2
1 BILL SUMMARY
2 This bill rezones the following parcels from A-2 (Agricultural Zone) to B-3 (Business
3 Scattered): Remainder Parcel No. 19-1-2 Estate Smith Bay, Nos. 1, 2, and 3 East End Quarter,
4 St. Thomas, Virgin Islands, consisting of approximately 13.460 U.S. acres as described on
5 survey drawing D9-9375-T019; Parcel No. 19-1-2-7 (Eastern portion) Estate Smith Bay, Nos.
6 1, 2, and 3 East End Quarter, St. Thomas, Virgin Islands, consisting of approximately 1.454
7 U.S. acres as described on survey drawing D9-9375-T019; and Parcel No. 19-1-2-7 (Western
8 portion) Estate Smith Bay, Nos. 1, 2, and 3 East End Quarter, St. Thomas, Virgin Islands,
9 consisting of approximately 0.846 U.S. acres as described on survey drawing D9-9375-T019.
