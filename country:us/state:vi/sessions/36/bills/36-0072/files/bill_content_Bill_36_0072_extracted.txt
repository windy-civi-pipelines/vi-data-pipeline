Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON BUDGET, APPROPRIATIONS AND FINANCE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
March 28, 2025
An Act appropriating $405,000 from the General Fund of the Treasury of the Virgin Islands to
the Virgin Islands Fire and Emergency Medical Services for the purchase of three ambulances
PROPOSED BY: Senator Ray Fonseca
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. The sum of $405,000 is appropriated from the General Fund of the
3 Treasury of the Government of the Virgin Islands to the Virgin Islands Fire and Emergency
4 Medical Services in the fiscal year ending September 30, 2025, for the purchase of three
5 ambulances: one for St. Croix, one for St. John and one for St. Thomas.
6 BILL SUMMARY
7 This bill appropriates $405,000 from the General Fund of the Treasury of the
8 Government of the Virgin Islands to the Virgin Islands Fire and Emergency Medical Services
9 to fund the purchase of three ambulances for St. Croix, St. John and St. Thomas.
10 BR25-0517/March 5, 2025/GC
