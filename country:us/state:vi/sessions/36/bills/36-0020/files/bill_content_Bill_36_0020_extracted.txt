Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON HOMELAND SECURITY, JUSTICE AND
PUBLIC SAFETY
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
February 27, 2025
An Act amending title 2 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 1, section 6 allowing the Police Chief of
the Legislature; the Sergeant-of-Arms; or any security officer of the Legislature; who has peace
officer status, to arrest a person who neglects or refuses to appear before the Legislature in
obedience to subpoena
PROPOSED BY: Marvin A. Blyden
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 2 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 1, section 6 is amended by inserting
3 “the Police Chief of the Legislature; the Sergeant-of-Arms of the Legislature; or any security
4 officer of the Legislature; who has peace officer status, or by” after “arrested by”.
5 BILL SUMMARY
6 This bill amends title 2 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 1, section 6 by allowing, in addition
7 to any member of the territorial Police Force, the Police Chief of the Legislature; the Sergeant-
8 of-Arms of the Legislature; or any security officer of the Legislature; who has peace officer
9 status, to arrest a person who neglects or refuses to appear before the Legislature in obedience
10 to subpoena.
11 BR25-0216/February 3, 2025/GC
