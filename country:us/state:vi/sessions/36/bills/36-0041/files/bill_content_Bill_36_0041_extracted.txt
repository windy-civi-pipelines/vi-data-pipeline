Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON BUDGET, APPROPRIATIONS AND FINANCE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
February 27, 2025
An Act amending sections 8 and 9 of Act No. 7267 by changing the authorized use of Matricula
22-a Estate Mars Hill, West End Quarter, St. Croix, Virgin Islands, and by reprogramming the
$2,000,000 appropriation
P ROPOSED BY: Senator Novelle E. Francis, Jr.
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. (a) Act No. 7267 is amended by striking section 9 in its entirety and
3 inserting the following new language:
4 “(a) The Department of Property and Procurement shall transfer title and ownership of
5 Matricula 22-a Estate Mars Hill, West End Quarter, St. Croix, Virgin Islands, consisting of
6 approximately 44.503 U.S. acres, as more fully described in OLG No. 5123, dated October 29,
7 1998, to the Virgin Islands Housing Finance Authority (“VIHFA”) for the purpose of
8 constructing mixed-income affordable housing.
9 (b) The $2,000,000 appropriated to the VIHFA in section 8 of Act No. 7267 to be used
10 for architectural mid engineering services and infrastructure work for a multipurpose indoor
11 complex is reprogrammed for VIHFA to use for the construction of mixed-income affordable
12 housing at Matricula 22-a Estate Mars Hill, West End Quarter, St. Croix, Virgin Islands.”
2
1 BILL SUMMARY
2 This bill amends sections 8 and 9 of Act No. 7267 by changing the authorized use of
3 Matricula 22-a Estate Mars Hill, West End Quarter, St. Croix, Virgin Islands from a
4 Multipurpose Indoor Complex to mixed-income affordable housing, and reprogramming the
5 $2,000,000 appropriated to the Virgin Islands Housing Finance Authority (“VIHFA”) for the
6 architectural and engineering services, and infrastructure work for the Multipurpose Indoor
7 Complex for the VIHFA to you use for the construction of mixed-income affordable housing.
8 BR25-0439/February 25, 2025/PFA
