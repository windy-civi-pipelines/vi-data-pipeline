Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON HOMELAND SECURITY, JUSTICE AND
PUBLIC SAFETY
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
February 27, 2025
An Act amending title 9 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 15, subchapter II, section 233 relating to
pawnbrokers to increase the fine for violating any law regulating pawnbrokers
PROPOSED BY: Senator Angel L. Bolques, Jr.
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 9 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 15, subchapter II, section 233 is
3 amended by striking “$50” and inserting “$250”.
4 BILL SUMMARY
5 This bill amends title 9 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 15, subchapter II, section 233 by
6 increasing the fine, from $50 to $250, for violating any law regulating pawnbrokers.
7 BR25-0021/February 3, 2025/GC
