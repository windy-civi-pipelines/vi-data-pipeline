Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON HOMELAND SECURITY, JUSTICE AND PUBLIC
SAFETY
06/25/2025-REPORTED OUT TO THE FLOOR
BILL NO. 36-0057
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
March 28, 2025
An Act amending title 5 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 314, section 3733, subsection (a) to
require automatic expungement of the arrest record and complaint or information when the
accused has been acquitted
PROPOSED BY: Senators Alma Francis Heyliger and Avery L. Lewis
Co-sponsors: Clifford A. Joseph, Sr., Ray Fonseca
and Milton E. Potter
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 5 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 314, section 3733, subsection (a) is
3 amended by striking paragraph (2) and by adding the following subsection (c):
4 “(c) Upon the acquittal of an accused, the Court shall enter an order expunging the
5 accused record of the arrest and the criminal complaint or information upon which the accused
6 was tried.”
7 BILL SUMMARY
8 This bill requires the Court enter an order expunging the arrest record and the complaint
9 or information upon which the accused was tried, if the accused was acquitted of the charges.
10 BR25-0298/March 18, 2025/SLR
