Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON HOUSING, TRANSPORTATION AND
TELECOMMUNICATIONS
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
February 27, 2025
An Act amending title 3 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 9, section 138 relating to the powers of
the Department of Public Works to provide for the operation of the Virgin Islands Transit
System
PROPOSED BY: Senator Avery L. Lewis
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 3 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 9, section 138, subsection (a) is
3 amended by designating paragraph (11) as paragraph (12) and inserting a new paragraph (11)
4 that reads as follows:
5 “(11) operate and maintain the Virgin Islands Transit System (VITRAN) and provide
6 public transportation services on St. Croix, St. Thomas, and St. John, seven days a week,
7 including weekends and holidays.”
8 BILL SUMMARY
9 The bill codifies the authority of the Department of Public Works to operate and maintain
10 the VITRAN and requires that VITRAN to provide transportation services seven days a week,
11 including weekends and holidays on St. Croix, St. Thomas, and St. John.
12 BR25-0154/February 7, 2025/AA
