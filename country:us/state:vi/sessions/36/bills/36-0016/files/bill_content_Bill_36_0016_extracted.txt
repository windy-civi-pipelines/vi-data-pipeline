Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON BUDGET, APPROPRIATIONS AND FINANCE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
February 27, 2025
An Act appropriating the sum of $800,000.00, from the Senior Citizens’ Center Revolving
Fund, to the Virgin Islands Department of Human Services for the general upkeep of senior
centers throughout the Territory
PROPOSED BY: Senator Avery L. Lewis
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. (a) The sum of $300,000 is appropriated in the fiscal year ending
3 September 30, 2026, from the Senior Citizens’ Center Revolving Fund, to the Virgin Islands
4 Department of Human Services, to be equally distributed to the following senior citizens’
5 community centers for general upkeep: Aldershille Senior Center; Richmond Senior Center;
6 Project Strive Center; Anna’s Retreat Senior Center; Adrian Senior Center; and St. Ursula’s
7 Senior Center.
8 (b) The sum of $500,000 is appropriated in the fiscal year ending September 30, 2026,
9 from the Senior Citizens’ Center Revolving Fund, to the Virgin Islands Department of Human
10 Services, to be equally distributed to the following senior citizens’ community centers for
11 general upkeep: Herbert Grigg Home for the Aged; Queen Louise Home for the Aged; and
12 Lucinda A. Millin Senior Citizens’ Homes.
13 (c) The funds appropriated in subsections (a) and (b) remain available until expended.
2
1 BILL SUMMARY
2 This bill appropriates the sum of $300,000.00, from the Senior Citizens’ Center
3 Revolving Fund to the Virgin Islands Department of Human Services, for the general upkeep
4 of Aldershille Senior Center, Richmond Senior Center, Project Strive Center, Anna’s Retreat
5 Senior Center, Adrian Senior Center, and St. Ursula’s Senior Center. This bill also appropriates
6 the sum of $500,000.00, from the Senior Citizens’ Center Fund to the Virgin Islands
7 Department of Human Services, for the general upkeep of Herbert Grigg Home for the Aged,
8 Queen Louise Home for the Aged, and Lucinda A. Millin Senior Citizens’ Homes.
