Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON GOVERNMENT OPERATIONS,
VETERANS AFFAIRS AND CONSUMER PROTECTION
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
August 26, 2025
An act amending title 3 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 16, section 274 by mandating that the
Virgin Islands Taxicab Commission implement an electronic payment system
PROPOSED BY: Senator Kurt A. Vialet
1 WHEREAS, the Virgin Islands Taxicab Commission currently oversees and regulates
2 the operation of taxicabs throughout the Virgin Islands;
3 WHEREAS, the modernization of transportation services is essential to ensuring,
4 reliable, efficient, and convenient service for residents and visitors;
5 WHEREAS, advances in financial technology have made electronic monetary payment
6 systems a secure, efficient, and convenient method for conducting transactions;
7 WHEREAS, the ability to pay for taxicab services electronically provides added
8 convenience for consumers, particularly tourists, who may not carry cash;
9 WHEREAS, electronic payments enhance accountability and transparency in financial
10 transactions for both drivers and the commission;
11 WHEREAS, requiring the Taxicab Commission to implement an electronic monetary
12 payment system aligns the Virgin Islands with industry standards across the United States and
13 internationally; and
2
1 WHEREAS, the modernization of payment methods in the transportation sector will
2 improve the territory's reputation as a progressive, visitor-friendly destination; Now, Therefore,
3 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
4 SECTION 1. Title 3 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 16, section 274 is amended by re-
5 designating subsections (i) and (j) as (j) and (k), respectively, and by adding the following new
6 subsection (i):
7 “(i) The Commission shall establish and operate an electronic monetary payment system
8 and establish conditions and requirements for its use.”
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective 120 days after enactment.
10 BILL SUMMARY
11 This bill provides that the Virgin Islands Taxicab Commission shall implement an
12 electronic payment system.
13 BR25-0782/August 25, 2025/SLR
