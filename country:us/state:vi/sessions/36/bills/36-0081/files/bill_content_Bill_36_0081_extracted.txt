Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON BUDGET, APPROPRIATIONS AND FINANCE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
May 8, 2025
An act amending title 21 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 2, section 103, by increasing the
compensation for nongovernment members of the Housing Finance Authority
PROPOSED BY: Senator Ray Fonseca
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Title 21 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 2, section 103 is amended in the first
3 paragraph by striking “$75” and inserting “$250”.
4 BILL SUMMARY
5 This bill amends title 21 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 2, section 103, by increasing the
6 compensation for nongovernment members of the Housing Finance Authority from $75 to
7 $250.
8 BR25-0364/April 16, 2025/NS
