Section 1:
COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE/FLOOR
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
July 23, 2025
An act amending Official Zoning Map No. STZ-4 to rezone Parcel No. 68A Estate Lindberg
Bay, No. 4A, Southside Quarter, St. Thomas from P (Public) to R-3 (Residential-Medium
Density)
PROPOSED BY: Senator Milton E. Potter, by Request of the Governor
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Pursuant to title 29 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 3, section 238, Official
3 Zoning Map No. STZ-4 for the island of St. Thomas is amended by changing the zoning
4 designation of Parcel No. 68A Estate Lindberg Bay No. 4A, Southside Quarter, St. Thomas
5 consisting of approximately 4.798 U.S. acres, as shown on survey drawing D9-775-T68 from
6 P (Public) to R-3 (Residential-Medium Density).
7 BILL SUMMARY
8 This bill rezones Parcel No. 68A Estate Lindberg Bay, No. 4A, Southside Quarter, St.
9 Thomas from P (Public) to R-3 (Residential-Medium Density)
10 BR25-0720/July 17, 2025/AA
