Section 1:
COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE/FLOOR
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
May 19, 2025
An act amending Official Zoning Map No. STZ-6, St. Thomas to establish a Planned Area
Development on the R-2 zoned Parcel Nos. 4i Remainder and 4J Remainder Estate St. Joseph
and Rosendahl, No. 4 Great Northside Quarter
PROPOSED BY: Senators Milton E. Potter, by Request of the Governor,
Marvin A. Blyden, and Clifford A Joseph, Sr.
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. Pursuant to title 29 Virgin Islands Code, chapter 3, section 238, Official
3 Zoning Map No. STZ-6 for the island of St. Thomas is amended by establishing a Planned Area
4 Development on the R-2 zoned Parcel Nos. 4i Remainder and 4J Remainder Estate St. Joseph
5 & Rosendahl, No. 4 Great Northside Quarter, St. Thomas, per the submitted file items 13A.
6 Engineering, 13B. Model, 13C. Renderings, and 23B Development Schedule.
7 BILL SUMMARY
8 This bill establishes a Planned Area Development on the R-2 zoned Parcel Nos. 4i
9 Remainder and 4J Remainder Estate St. Joseph & Rosendahl, No. 4 Great Northside Quarter,
10 St. Thomas.
11 BR25-0629/May 8, 2025/PFA
