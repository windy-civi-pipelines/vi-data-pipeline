Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON BUDGET, APPROPRIATIONS AND FINANCE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
March 28, 2025
An Act appropriating the sum of $400,000 from the Tourism Advertising Revolving Fund to
the Virgin Islands Taxicab Commission for the implementation of technological upgrades
P ROPOSED BY: Senator Angel L. Bolques, Jr.
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. (a) Notwithstanding the use restrictions in 33 V.I.C. ยง3072(c), there is
3 appropriated in the fiscal year ending September 30, 2025, the sum of $400,000 from the
4 Tourism Advertising Revolving Fund to the Virgin Islands Taxicab Commission for the
5 implementation of technological upgrades, including the digitization of paper records and files
6 and collaborating with B.I.T. to advise on the most suitable equipment and software for the
7 technological upgrades.
8 (b) The funds appropriated in section 1 remain available until expended.
9 BILL SUMMARY
10 This bill appropriates the sum of $400,000 from the Tourism Advertising Revolving
11 Fund to the Virgin Islands Taxicab Commission for the implementation of technological
12 upgrades including the digitization of paper records and files and collaborating with B.I.T. to
13 advise on the most suitable equipment and software for the technological upgrades.
