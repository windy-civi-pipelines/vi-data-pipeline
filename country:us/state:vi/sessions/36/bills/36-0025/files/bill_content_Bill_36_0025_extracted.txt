Section 1:
COMMITTEE ON BUDGET, APPROPRIATIONS AND FINANCE
Thirty-Sixth Legislature of the Virgin Islands
February 27, 2025
An Act appropriating $90,000 from the General Fund of the Treasury of the Government of the
Virgin Islands to the Virgin Islands Department of Human Services to purchase all-in-one
mobile health, wellness, and personal safety devices for Virgin Islands senior citizens and other
vulnerable residents, and pay the annual service associated with maintenance of the devices
PROPOSED BY: Senator Ray Fonseca
1 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the Virgin Islands:
2 SECTION 1. (a) The sum of $75,000 is appropriated from the General Fund of the
3 Treasury of the Government of the Virgin Islands in the fiscal year ending September 30, 2025,
4 to the Virgin Islands Department of Human Services to purchase Numera® Libris® 2mPERS
5 all-in-one mobile health, wellness, and personal safety devices.
6 (b) The sum of $15,000 is appropriated from the General Fund of the Treasury of the
7 Virgin Islands in the fiscal year ending September 30, 2025, to the Virgin Islands to the Virgin
8 Islands Department of Human Services to pay the annual service fee for the Numera® Libris®
9 2mPERS all-in-one mobile health, wellness, and personal safety devices.
10 (c) The funds remain available until expended.
2
1 BILL SUMMARY
2 This bill appropriates $90,000 from the General Fund of the Treasury of the Government
3 of the Virgin Islands to the Virgin Islands Department of Human Services to purchase all-in-
4 one mobile health, wellness, and personal safety devices, and pay the annual service fee
5 associated with maintenance of the devices.
6 BR25-0246/February 25, 2025/SLR
